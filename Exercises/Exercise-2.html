Exploring the World One Adventure at a Time

Destination


Santorini, Greece

Nestled in the Aegean Sea, Santorini offers a picturesque escape with its breathtaking sunsets and charming architecture.

Places to Visit:

  • Oia Village
  • Fira
  • Akrotiri Archaeological Site
  • Red Beach
  • Santo Wines Winery

Things to Do:

  • Watch the sunset in Oia
  • Explore the narrow streets of Fira
  • Visit the volcanic ruins at Akrotiri
  • Relax at the unique Red Beach
  • Taste local wines at Santo Wines Winery

Itinerary:

Day Activities
Day 1 Arrival, explore Fira
Day 2 Visit Oia, watch the sunset
Day 3 Explore Akrotiri, relax at Red Beach
Day 4 Wine tasting at Santo Wines Winery
Day 5 Departure
